Safety

Encore Jets does not own or operate aircraft or employ flight personnel. Instead, we exclusively work with our extensive global network of approved charter operators. Each operator is FAA Part 135 certified to ensure the highest level of safety for our clients. In addition, Encore Jets requires Wyvern and Argus safety minimums. Encore Jets currently reviews all brokered charters prior to departure to confirm that certain safety protocols and standards are passed.
